The EMBL PhD Symposium has been arranged by EMBL PhD students with the aim of creating an occasion for connecting young researchers and high-profile scientists. The focus of this year's Symposium is on the topic "The Power of Many: Collective Behaviour Across Scales".

Although the symposium is primarily directed towards PhD students, everybody is welcome to participate in it!

The venue for the symposium is EMBL Heidelberg, located at Meyerhofstraße 1, 69117 Heidelberg, Germany. Remote participation through virtual means is also available.

Registration fees are 200€ for Bachelor/Master students, 300€ for PhD students and 400€ for Academic participants. The fee for online participation is 25€, independent of your academic status. Apart from the talks, on-site fees include catering (coffee breaks, lunch, and dinner) and networking events with food and drinks. The registration fees do not include expenses for accommodation, however EMBL-recommended hotels can be found here.

Registration is possible until October 6th, 2023.
