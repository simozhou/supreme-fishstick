The EMBL PhD Symposium has been arranged by EMBL PhD students with the aim of creating an occasion for connecting young researchers and high-profile scientists. The focus of this year's Symposium is on the topic "The Power of Many: Collective Behaviour Across Scales".

Although the symposium is primarily directed towards PhD students, everybody is welcomed to participate in it!

The venue for the symposium is EMBL Heidelberg, located at Meyerhofstraße 1, 69117 Heidelberg, Germany. Remote participation through virtual means is also available.

The registration costs 300€ for PhD students, 200€ for Bachelor's and Master's students, and 400€ for other academics. Online participation is 25€, indepent of the participant's status.

Certainly! Individuals who have registered to attend the symposium in person are encouraged to submit their abstracts, specifying their preference for either a talk or a poster presentation. The organising committee will review the submitted abstracts after the deadline and select the participants for the flash talks.

Abstracts can only be submitted by registered applicants, and the link for abstract submission is provided in the confirmation email.

While we encourage abstract submissions, we don’t require abstracts to participate in the symposium.

Do not hesitate to reach out to phdsymposium23.participants@embl.de for any inquiries or concerns you may have.
