FAQ

What is the EMBL PhD symposium about?

The EMBL PhD Symposium has been arranged by EMBL PhD students with the aim of creating an occasion for connecting young researchers and high-profile scientists. The focus of this year's Symposium is on the topic "The Power of Many: Collective Behaviour Across Scales".

Who can attend the symposium?

Although the symposium is primarily directed towards PhD students, everybody is welcome to participate in it!

Where is the symposium being held?

The venue for the symposium is EMBL Heidelberg, located at Meyerhofstraße 1, 69117 Heidelberg, Germany. Remote participation through virtual means is also available.

How much does it cost?

Registration fees are 200€ for Bachelor/Master students, 300€ for PhD students and 400€ for Academic participants. The fee for online participation is 25€, independent of your academic status. Apart from the talks, on-site fees include catering (coffee breaks, lunch, and dinner) and networking events with food and drinks. The registration fees do not include expenses for accommodation, however hotels recommended by EMBL can be found here.

Until when can I register?

Registration is possible until October 6th, 2023.

Can I submit an abstract for a talk and/or poster presentation?

Certainly! Individuals who have registered to attend the symposium in person are encouraged to submit their abstracts, specifying their preference for either a talk or a poster presentation. The organising committee will review the submitted abstracts after the deadline and select the participants for the flash talks. If your abstract is selected for a poster, you will also have the opportunity to give a one minute/one slide introduction in the main auditorium before the poster session. Online participants will also have the opportunity to present posters.

Is there a deadline for submitting abstracts for a talk and/or poster presentation?

Yes, the deadline for abstract submission is 25th of August, 2023.

How do I submit an abstract?

Abstracts can only be submitted by registered applicants, and the link for abstract submission is provided in the confirmation email.

Do I need to submit an abstract to participate in the symposium?

While we encourage abstract submissions, we don’t require abstracts to participate in the symposium.

Is there a conference hotel? How do I make a reservation?

The registration fee does not cover accommodation expenses, but EMBL-recommended hotels are listed here.

How can I reach EMBL by public transportation from downtown Heidelberg?

Please visit the travel information page for details.

Will the presentations be recorded and available for viewing after the symposium?

We are using an event platform for this conference. This includes access to all livestreamed talks, a video library of recorded talks during and after the event (two weeks), and the opportunity to submit questions for all registered participants.

How can I contact the conference organisers if I have additional questions or concerns?

Do not hesitate to reach out to phdsymposium23@embl.de for any inquiries or concerns you may have.

What is the cancellation policy?

Please refer to the General Terms and Conditions for the cancellation policy. In urgent cases you can contact us at phdsymposium23@embl.de.
